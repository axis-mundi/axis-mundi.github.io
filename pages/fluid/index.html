Fluid
Fluid is a study of dynamic motion as an ever-changing form. With lights encapsulated in unique scalloped-like cut-out frames, the triptych features three interactive experiences that capture fluid's movement from a microscopic to macroscopic level. The first experience examines fluid in terms of molecular interactions as well as its reactions to changes in environment. The second study experiments with digital fluid simulation, and the third representation of fluid revolves around rain, an everyday natural phenomenon which often reminds one of nostalgia. With sensors set up on individual canvases, the tension of experience shifts as viewers move from one to another canvas and intensifies as they get close to the piece.
